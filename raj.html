About E-vehicle

An EV is defined as a vehicle that can be powered by an electric motor that draws electricity from a battery and is capable of being charged from an external source. An EV includes both a vehicle that can only be powered by an electric motor that draws electricity from a battery (all-electric vehicle) and a vehicle that can be powered by an electric motor that draws electricity from a battery and by an internal combustion engine (plug-in hybrid electric vehicle). read more

Motivation to start comes from:

Many times we have seen that on the long highways There are only petrol pumps and the mechanic shop for the vehicles which run on either diesel or petrol. But now we are proceeding towards the e vehicle which runs upon Electricity . But the main concern is that,there are very less charging station for those e-vehicle this create a boundation on the driver to choose cars which runs on fuels. So to resolve this problem we have made An application called "flying batteries". Through this application whenever a driver was on a long journey and he is unable to find any charging station then he will visit our site and mention the details of his car and the battery he required. After that our response team will immediately coordinate with the nearby shops and provide the batteries ,But if in the conditions of unavailability of batteries nearby we will deliver the battery by drone within half an hour.
